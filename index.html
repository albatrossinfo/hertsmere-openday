The Future of Practice

Our 26-bay floodlit driving range is powered by Toptracer Range technology, the same ball-tracking tech used on TV broadcasts. It transforms a standard practice session into an interactive experience.

  • Track ball speed, distance, and curve instantly.
  • Play virtual golf on world-famous courses.
  • Fun games like 'Go Fish' and 'Closest to Pin' for kids and families.
  • Heated bays and waiter service from Script Restaurant.

A Modern Classic

Designed by renowned architect Donald Steel, our 18-hole course offers a fair but challenging test for golfers of all abilities. The parkland layout weaves through mature trees and natural water features.

Year-Round Play

Exceptional drainage ensures the course remains playable on main greens throughout the winter months.

Tee Variety

Multiple tee options allow you to tailor the length of the course to your ability level.

Script Restaurant & Bar

More than just a "clubhouse", Script is a destination in itself. With panoramic floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the 18th green, it's the perfect spot for post-round drinks or a Sunday roast with the family.
